Alter Source

Overview

So, the mod Alter Source is basically just a sandbox mod. But don't think it's those crappy ahh sandbox mods with the spawnmenu from a JBMod addon.
This one is different, it has Lua support, a better physics gun (colorable since 1.0!) and updates. The devlogs are even quicker.

You'll probably like it, or not, i have no idea. According to your tastes.

Installation

  1. Download the latest version of Alter Source from ModDB
  2. Extract the files to your sourcemods folder (ex.: C:/Steam/SteamApps/sourcemods)

    3. PS: the bin folder outside the altersrc folder is for mappers and stuff. Don't extract that if you're not going to map for Alter Source.

  3. Restart Steam and enjoy!
